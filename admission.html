Chrysalis ADMISSIONS PROCESS:

STEP 1: Contact the school via email or cellphone to book an interview during school hours. The prospective pupil is expected to accompany the parent to the interview.
STEP 2: Interview and tour of the facilities - bring along:
  1. your child's birth certificate
  2. parents' ID documents
  3. child's most recent school report
At this stage, a second appointment will be made to complete an Admissions Assessment (Literacy and Numeracy), to determine if Chrysalis would be the right academic fit for the child. This second appointment is confirmed upon receipt of a R500 Application fee, and does not apply to pre-school or Grade R pupils.
STEP 3: Upon successful completion of the Admissions Assessment, the parent may be invited to complete the Registration Forms. Registration is complete upon receipt of a non-refundable R2000.00 Registration Fee and a non-refundable Development fund payment (amount available upon request).
