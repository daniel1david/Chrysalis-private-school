Parent Involvement

Open Communication

Parents are invited to examine their child's work at any stage. Quarterly parent-teacher meetings will be held. Parents are encouraged to maintain regular, open communication with their child's teacher. All communications are to be directed via the Principal.

One Hour Per Month

In the spirit of UNITY, GROWTH and EXCELLENCE we request that EVERY parent donates ONE hour of their time EVERY MONTH to Chrysalis (from February to November).

Working parents: We understand time is limited. By planning ahead, there are many opportunities to contribute without taking time off work.
Please note: These opportunities may not be used as a platform to disseminate religious material.
