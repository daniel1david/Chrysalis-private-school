School Uniform Policy

Simple, practical, and proudly Chrysalis.

  • School Golf Shirts: Must be ordered directly from the Principal.
  • Pants: Long or short denims, chinos, or cargo pants in blue denim, stone, or khaki.
  • Shoes: Sensible, dark-colored takkies or closed shoes.
  • Winter Accessories: Bottle green scarves, gloves, or beanies.
  • Rain Jacket: Bottle green dri-mac (available at PEP).
  • Sunscreen: Parents must provide daily.
  • NO leggings, skirts, or cropped tops allowed.
  • Jewellery & Nail Polish: Permitted if discreet and well-maintained.
  • Hair: Neatly groomed, must not obstruct vision.

Uniform fosters equality, pride, and readiness to learn.
