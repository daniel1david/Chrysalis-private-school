Uniform

    The Private School has a specific uniform

  • School golf shirts are to be ordered from the Principal.
  • Long or short denims. chinos/cargo pants (blue denim, stone or khaki).
  • Sensible, dark coloured shoes, e.g. takkies.
  • Bottle green scarves, gloves or beanies in winter.
  • Bottle green dri-macs can be purchased from PEP by parents.
  • Please provide sunscreen for your child.
  • NO LEGGINGS OR SKIRTS.
  • Jewellery and nail polish may be worn on the condition that it is discrete and well-maintained.
  • Hair is to be neatly groomed and may not hang in the pupil's eyes.
