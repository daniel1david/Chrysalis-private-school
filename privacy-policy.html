Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 16, 2025

1. Introduction

Chrysalis Private School ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website chrysalisprivateschool.co.za or interact with our services.

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and any other details you provide via contact forms or admission inquiries.
  • Non-Personal Information: Browser type, IP address, pages visited, and time spent on our site (via Google Analytics or similar tools).
  • Student & Parent Data: Collected only during enrollment and stored securely in compliance with POPIA (Protection of Personal Information Act, South Africa).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide school information
  • Process admission applications

  • Send occasional updates (with your consent)

4. Data Protection & Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only. All data is stored in secure, encrypted systems.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service providers (e.g., email platforms, cloud storage) under strict confidentiality
  • Legal authorities when required by law

6. Your Rights (POPIA Compliance)

Under South African law, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Object to processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Contact us at angiemostert@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

9. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent. All student data is handled with strict parental oversight.
