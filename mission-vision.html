Mission Statement

To foster a love of learning amongst self-motivated, independent learners who will be given the tools to attain an internationally recognised school-leaving certificate.

Chrysalis Aims To:

  • Provide a loving, encouraging, joyous, caring environment thereby fostering a lifelong love of learning both in terms of academics and God-given talents.
  • Provide a constantly monitored, carefully designed programme of instruction, customised wherever feasible and possible to the needs of each individual child.
  • Provide the services of a dedicated, passionate, compassionate teaching faculty.
  • Endeavour to improve Chrysalis facilities in order to better meet the needs of our students.
  • Afford our students the opportunity to utilise their talent to build positively into the community.
